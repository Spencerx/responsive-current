Provide Feedback or Report Concerns on Disability Access

Syracuse University values diversity, is committed to inclusion, and seeks to provide an accessible, welcoming environment for all people, including those with disabilities. Your feedback is essential to SU’s continuing effort to move beyond ADA compliance and toward seamless access.

If you have questions, comments, requests, needs or complaints regarding your living situation, your studies, your work at Syracuse University and/or about an experience with University employees, events, programs, services or facilities, please provide the information requested below. It will be shared with people who have a need to know in order to address your concern(s). You may provide information anonymously by leaving the personal information blank. To contact someone directly, call the Office of Equal Opportunity, Inclusion, and Resolution Services at 443-0211.
